Key Takeaways

  • Padding increases space inside the box (pushes content away from border)
  • Border surrounds content + padding (the visible edge)
  • Margin controls spacing between elements (outside the border)
  • Content Area = where your text lives (the colored area)
  • Total size = content + padding + border (margin is outside)
